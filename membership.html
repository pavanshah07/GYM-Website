Does your gym or fitness center see an increase in memberships at the beginning of each year? How about after a huge promotion? New membership opportunities for gyms, fitness centers, and health clubs are abundant. Whether it’s the start of the year or the end of a membership promotion, it’s time to start thinking about your membership cards. Is your membership card program working for your facility? The membership cards you offer are a part of the overall experience. People expect an easy-to-use card that they can use regularly without concern. When it causes frustration, it can decrease the member’s overall satisfaction with your business. Membership cards should be a reputation improvement, not a detraction.

Occasionally, we get asked if CARDSource offers RFID chip cards. We do have RFID capabilities, though many times, barcode, magnetic stripe, and variable data cards are quicker to produce at a lower price—which helps you keep overhead costs down. Plus, there are many custom shapes and sizes available. In addition, PVC lamination is imageable, which allows these durable cards to be easily imprinted with custom information.
